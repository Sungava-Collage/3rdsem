Bachelor of Business Studies (BBS)

Overview

  • Equip students with conceptual knowledge of business and administration
  • Develop attitudes, abilities, and practical skills for business management
  • Encourage entrepreneurial capabilities
  • Prepare foundation for higher studies and careers in management

Minimum General Requirements

  • An academic year consists of a minimum of 150 teaching days
  • Total instructional hours per year: 615
  • Minimum of five periods each teaching day

Attendance, Evaluation, and Grading System

  • Final evaluation through annual examination
  • Minimum 70% attendance required
  • Grading system: Third Division (35%), Second Division (45%), First Division (60%), Distinction (75%)

Methods of Instruction

Combination of lectures, group discussions, problem-solving exercises, guest lectures, practical work, and fieldwork.

Encouraged use of the case method of instruction.

Eligibility

Candidates must have successfully completed 10+2 in business/commerce or equivalent with at least D+ in each subject.

Curricular Structure

Curriculum comprises:

  1. Allied areas of business
  2. Core business studies
  3. Specialization areas

Curricular structure divided into four years with specific courses each year.

Careers

  • Administrative Assistants and Secretaries
  • Administrative Services Managers
  • Financial Analysts
  • Financial Clerks
  • Financial Managers
  • Human Resources Assistants
  • Human Resources Managers
